Personal Lease

For personal lease, the following are the initial payment which the client needs to prepare:


  • - Booking fee (which will be used as 1st month rent) - 1 month’s rental equivalent
  • - Security Deposit – 1 to 2 months’ rental equivalent (depending on the length of the lease)
  • - Stamp Duty Fee – borne by the tenant
  • - Realtor’s commission (for rent below S$4000) - one month’s rental equivalent

Documents required:


  • - EP Approval document
  • - Copy of Passport

It is recommended to open a bank account in Singapore. This will help to facilitate the payment without delay when a property is identified.
